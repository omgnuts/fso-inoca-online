KEYBOARD COMMANDS
Tab - wields weapons and tools. Use it to attack, to fish, to mine... etc. You must have an item equipped in order to use it.
Arrow Keys - controls the direction of your character or vessel. Right, left, up or down.
Ctrl - enables you to pick up items. You must be standing next to the item to grab it. Also enables you to board/unboard vessels.
Alt -allows you to change direction while standing still, when held down in conjunction with the corresponding arrow key of the direction you wish to face.
